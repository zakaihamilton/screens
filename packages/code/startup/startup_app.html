Cannot Load Screens Environment

Error: __error__

Common Reasons: Popups and Third-party cookies are needed by Google to login.
Enable Third-party cookies and popups for Screens Environment to load.
Chrome Mobile:
1. Open the Chrome browser.
2. Tap the Settings icon in the upper right corner.
3. Under Advanced, tap Content Settings.
4. Tap Block Pop-ups, and tap the toggle so that it turns off blocking and allows pop-ups.
